History

In year 1961, Krushnaji Joshi ,Nanasaheb Garge ,Eaknathji Ekbote,Murlidhar Hardas,Govind Parashare established "Lokmanya Shikshan Prasarak Mandal, Nashik". As Nashik city was growing at pace & there was need of a school for the educational requirement of the society.
